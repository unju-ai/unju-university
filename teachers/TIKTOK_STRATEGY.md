## Video Production

### Automation Pipeline

```
1. Script Generation (AI)
   - Teacher personality prompt
   - Topic/outline
   - Generate script

2. Voiceover (ElevenLabs)
   - Each teacher has unique voice
   - Text-to-speech from script

3. Visuals (After Effects / Motion Graphics)
   - Code snippets
   - Charts/graphs
   - Animations
   - Teacher avatar (optional)

4. Editing (CapCut / Premiere)
   - Add captions
   - Pacing (keep it punchy)
   - Background music
   - Transitions

5. Publishing (TikTok API)
   - Schedule posts
   - Auto-hashtag
   - Cross-post to Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
```

## Metrics & Goals

### Growth Targets (6 months)

**Each Teacher Account:**
- 10,000 followers (Month 1)
- 50,000 followers (Month 3)
- 100,000 followers (Month 6)

**Engagement:**
- 5%+ engagement rate
- 10%+ save rate (valuable content)
- 50+ comments per video (active community)

**Conversions:**
- 1% click-through to website
- 10% of website visitors start Level 0
- 5% of Level 0 completions enroll in Level 1

### Content KPIs

**Per Video:**
- Watch time >50% (viewers finish)
- Completion rate >60%
- Share rate >2%

**Per Teacher:**
- 2-3 videos/day minimum
- 1 viral hit (500k+ views) per month
- 10+ comments per video

## Community Management

### Comment Strategy

**Respond to:**
- Questions (educate in comments)
- Corrections (acknowledge, update)
- Great insights (amplify)
- Haters (with facts, not emotion)

**Ignore:**
- Spam
- Obvious trolls
- Off-topic

**Escalate to video:**
- Frequently asked questions
- Interesting debates
- Student suggestions

### Discord Integration

- Share TikToks in #content channel
- Get student feedback before posting
- Feature Discord members in videos
- Create feedback loop

## Legal & Compliance

**Disclaimers (always include):**
- Trading: "Not financial advice. Trading is risky."
- Smart Contracts: "Code examples for education only. Audit before mainnet."
- AI Agents: "Use responsibly. Test thoroughly."

**FTC Compliance:**
- Disclose sponsorships (if any)
- Clear when content is educational vs promotional
- Honest about results ("your mileage may vary")
